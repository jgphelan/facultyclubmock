Food Allergies & Dietary Accommodations

The Brown Faculty Club is committed to providing safe and enjoyable dining experiences for all our guests.

Our Accommodation Policy

The Brown Faculty Club can safely and reasonably guide event planners in booking events and making menu selections that accommodate their guests. In most instances, food allergies and intolerances can be managed independently at events.

Important Notice:
  • The Brown Faculty Club does not label all allergens at functions
  • We cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment
  • Guests with severe allergies should exercise appropriate caution

Requesting Accommodations

Event planners should submit a formal request to the Brown Faculty Club Sales & Event Manager for each guest requiring a meal accommodation.

Please include:

  • Specific allergen or dietary restriction
  • Severity of the allergy or restriction
  • Number of guests requiring accommodation
  • Event date and details

Contact for Accommodations

For all dietary accommodation requests, please contact:

Sales & Event Manager
Phone: 401-863-3023
Email: events@brown.edu

We recommend submitting accommodation requests at least 48 hours in advance of your event.

  • Dining Privileges — Access to lunch service and special dining events
  • Event Booking — Priority access to private event spaces
  • Reciprocal Club Access — Visiting privileges at university clubs worldwide
  • Special Events — Invitations to member-only gatherings and celebrations
  • Guest Privileges — Ability to bring guests to club facilities
  • Community — Connection with Brown's academic community

House Rules & Expectations

All members are expected to review and adhere to the Brown Faculty Club rules and regulations.

  • Reservations required for lunch service (parties of 6 or more)
  • Business casual dress code during lunch hours
  • Mobile phone use should be courteous and discrete
  • Members are responsible for their guests' conduct
  • Event bookings must be made through the Club office

Reciprocal Club Information

  • Who is eligible for reciprocity? Active Brown Faculty Club members in good standing with current membership dues paid.
  • How do I use reciprocal privileges? Contact the reciprocal club directly to make reservations and mention your Brown Faculty Club membership.
  • What proof is needed? You'll need your active Brown Faculty Club membership ID and a valid photo ID when visiting reciprocal clubs.
  • Are there any restrictions? Some clubs may have specific policies or limitations for reciprocal members. Check with each club directly.

Directions

The Brown Faculty Club is conveniently located in the heart of Brown University's campus on Magee Street. The building is easily accessible by foot, bicycle, or car.

Parking: Limited street parking is available on Magee Street and nearby campus streets. Visitors may also use nearby campus parking facilities.

Public Transportation: RIPTA bus routes serve the Brown University area. The Club is within walking distance of the main campus and downtown Providence.
