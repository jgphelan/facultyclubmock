Food Allergies & Dietary Accommodations

We are committed to meeting the needs of guests who have special dietary restrictions and recognize that many guests may adhere to vegetarian or vegan diets.

Our Commitment

The Brown Faculty Club can safely and reasonably guide event planners in booking events and making menu selections that accommodate their guests. In most instances, food allergies and intolerances can be managed independently at events.

Important Notes:

  • The Brown Faculty Club does not label all allergens at functions
  • With advance notice, we can generally accommodate guests who must avoid common food allergens and gluten
  • We are unable to accommodate individual dietary preferences (these should be communicated directly to the event planner)
  • We cannot accommodate therapeutic diets (diabetic, cardiac, renal, etc.)

Request an Accommodation

Event planners should submit a formal request to the Brown Faculty Club Sales & Event Manager for each guest requiring a meal accommodation.

Timeline Requirement Accommodation requests must be submitted no later than four (4) business days from the event date. Guests should identify themselves to the Manager at the event.

Eligibility

Membership in the Faculty Club is open to:

  • Brown Faculty and Staff — All full-time and part-time employees
  • Clinical Faculty — Application to be completed in Workday
  • Alumni — Brown University graduates
  • Parents — Parents of Brown students
  • Graduate Students — Currently enrolled graduate students
  • Faculty and Staff from Other Institutions
  • Friends of Brown — Supporters and friends of the university

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is eligible for reciprocity? Active Brown Faculty Club members in good standing with current membership dues paid.
  • How long does processing take? Reciprocity requests are typically processed within 5-7 business days, though international verifications may take longer.
  • What proof is needed? You'll need your active Brown Faculty Club membership ID and a valid photo ID when visiting reciprocal clubs.
